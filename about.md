Behind every business question is data. I focus on turning that data into answers people can actually use.

Most of my work centres on building practical, reliable reporting that helps teams understand what's happening, why it's happening, and where to focus next. I enjoy working with messy or incomplete data and turning it into something clean, structured, and usable.

I primarily work with Power BI, SQL, and data modelling to design scalable reporting solutions — building semantic models, shaping data for usability, and creating dashboards that support self-service without sacrificing accuracy or trust.

My approach is grounded in a few key principles:

- Good reporting lets people explore confidently
- Metrics only matter if everyone is working from the same definition
- Dashboards reflect how decisions are made
- Clean design matters — but so does performance and long-term maintainability

This portfolio is a mix of structured projects and working examples — some are fully developed analyses, while others capture specific patterns, techniques, or solutions I've built along the way.
